# Personalized Story Generator

Generate custom audio stories for your child's Toniebox Creative-Tonie!

## Features

- **Personalized**: Stories feature your child's name as the main character
- **Age-appropriate**: Content tailored to your child's age group
- **Multiple themes**: Pirates, Space, Dinosaurs, Princesses, and more
- **Custom themes**: Enter any theme you want
- **High-quality audio**: Natural-sounding voices
- **Free**: No account required, completely free to use

## How to Use

1. Enter your child's name
2. Select their age group
3. Choose a theme (or enter a custom one)
4. Pick a voice
5. Click "Generate Story"
6. Download the MP3
7. Upload to your Creative-Tonie via [my.tonies.com](https://my.tonies.com) or the mytonies app

## Limits

- 10 stories per day per user
- Stories are 5-20 minutes depending on age group

## Technology

- **Story Generation**: Powered by Groq (Llama 3.3)
- **Text-to-Speech**: Microsoft Edge TTS (free, high-quality neural voices)
- **Interface**: Gradio

## Privacy

- No data is stored
- No accounts required
- Stories are generated on-demand and not saved
